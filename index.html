Jimi Hendrix

Jimi Hendrix was a famous guitarist known for his work in the Jimi Hendrix Experience. He is often cited as one of the most influential guitarists of all time. His usage of guitar pedals and effects combined with his talent made him very popular.

The Equipment

Jimi Hendrix's sound came from his usage of Marshall amps and Fendor Stratocasters. He also utilized many guitar pedals and effects such as the ones you can hear in songs such as 'All Along the Watcher'.

Fendor Stratocaster

The Fendor Stratocaster is a famous model of guitar known for it's blues-like sound. It was created by Leo Fender.

Legacy and Influence

Jimi Hendrix has inspired many musicians and to this day is highly regarded amongst the music community.

He was one of the first musicians to use distortion and other effects to achieve his sound.

He was also known for his electric and fluid guitar playing.

He recorded many albums with his band and also many legendary live performances.
